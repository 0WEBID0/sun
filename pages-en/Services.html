Our services

The services you provide!

We at Shomos Al Sharq Company offer our clients a variety of engineering and related services and consultations according to the latest methods and the most distinguished methods to suit all requirements and needs. Including:

Designing safety and fire-fighting plans and approving them at the Civil Defense
Supervising the implementation of safety and fire-fighting works
Issuing a building safety and electrical wiring safety certificate
Issuing technical reports for the Salama website and Baladi website
Design emergency and evacuation plans
Issuing a certificate of conformity and completion of work for projects
Preparing reports and engineering studies for existing buildings and approving them with the Civil Defense

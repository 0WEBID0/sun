Safety consulting and fire protection engineering

Issuing construction safety reports

The technical construction safety report is a requirement from municipalities and civil defense to obtain a license and operate commercial facility buildings, which is what prompted us at Reed Office to provide the service of issuing construction safety reports, after ensuring that the security plans match what is actually present in the building.

Design of electrical safety systems

Reed Office provides electrical safety systems design service, which includes designing fire alarm systems, voice evacuation, backup lighting, grounding, lightning rods, and backup generator loads.

All these services, details, and more make Reed one of the best safety systems installation companies in Saudi Arabia.

Review, approve and design safety plans

At Reed Office, we provide the service of reviewing, approving and designing safety plans for commercial establishments in all sectors, which are a requirement of government agencies, and we verify the existence of safety systems to preserve property and protect buildings and facilities from exposure to fires.

We prepare our plans after ensuring that the designs comply with the Saudi building codes regulations, and issuing a civil defense license and approved safety plans.

Installation of fire protection systems

A specialized and experienced engineering team supervises the installation of fire protection systems inside buildings, which includes installing a fire extinguishing system, installing a fire alarm system, installing emergency doors, and installing a mechanical extinguishing system.

Issuing quantity inventory reports

Through a visit to the building site by the engineer in charge at Reedโs office, the quantities of mechanical and electrical safety systems are determined and a report is then issued on those quantities.

Issuing a safety report and obtaining accreditation

You will not be able to obtain a license and operate commercial establishment buildings without obtaining a certificate of receipt of safety systems.

At Reed, we provide the service of extracting a safety report with the highest efficiency and quality by reviewing the systems, ensuring their effectiveness and compliance with the approved plans, and issuing a certificate based on that.

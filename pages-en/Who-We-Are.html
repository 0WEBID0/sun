Who are we?

About us in brief

Shams Al Sharq Company is a leading company specialized in the field of engineering consultancy. Providing the best services and solutions to the most complex technical problems in the field of security, safety and civil defense. Our practical and technical experience, technological resources, and comprehensive diagnostic, analytical and material capabilities in this field enable us to provide our customers with the most comprehensive and efficient services and solutions.

Shams Al-Sharq Company is also specialized in providing fire prevention and protection systems and occupational safety services. It is a company accredited by the Civil Defense in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and has full security licenses, as well as accreditation from the Corps of Engineers operating in the Kingdom.

Shamous Al-Sharq Company has worked and is still working with clients in projects that extend across most regions of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Our team has distinguished itself and our reputation has also distinguished itself in providing superior results at competitive prices through a network of experts, consultants and employees who spare no effort in providing the best of their skills and experience. From the first steps of projects until their optimal completion
